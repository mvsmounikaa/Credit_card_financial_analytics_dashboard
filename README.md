# Credit_card_financial_analytics_dashboard

Credit Card Analytics Dashboard using PowerBI:
Developed a data-driven dashboard to analyze credit card transactions, customer behavior, and overall business performance.

Key Insights:
Revenue increased by 28.8% WoW,
Total revenue reached 57M with 46M transactions,
Blue & Silver cards contribute 93% of transactions,
TX, NY, CA contribute 68% of overall revenue,
Activation rate: 57.5% | Delinquency: 6.06%

Tech stack:
Power BI, SQL, DAX, Data Visualization
